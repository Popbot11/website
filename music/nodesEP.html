a fun 5-track EP that I wrote in late 2021 and finished in 2022. The EP didn't really have any sort of unified vision; it was just 5 tracks that I made at around the same time. I'm still quite proud of the first half of decagonite, I think the mixdown is one of my best. I'm also fond of the rhythmic content in schlonkus, as well as the form and structure in the last track.
Another interesting bit of "lore" is that Mammatus Clouds won an honorable mention up in a national competetion for electronic composers in high school, which is neat.
