battery muncher is a m4l device that can create ridiculous uncontrolled digital distorted noise textures out of the input. The idea for the device originated from conversations with my friend jasko (RIP, legend), who iirc will sometimes use desmos to generate wavetables mathematically. Be careful with the feedback, make sure to use a clipper or limmiter at the end of the chain so that you don't break anything. with certain settings the device can produce insane DC.

i'd try and explain what each param does, but tbh i dont remember, and you could probably figure it out just as well as i could. this isn't polished or tested or anything, so theres probably bugs and the UX is ass. but its fun to mess with so. yeah. enjoy :)
