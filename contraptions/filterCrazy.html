A stupid simple utility I made for my friend emily in a very small ammount of time, maybe 5 or 10 minutes. It modulates a biquad filter's frequency at audio rate with the LFO shape defined in the envelope editor. Because biquad filters are silly, they more or less explode when you modulate them fast enough.

Hopefully this is fun to mess around with despite its extreme simplicity.
